Mailing Address

The central mailing address for Georgia State University is:

P.O. Box 3965
Atlanta, GA 30302-3965.

To send mail to a specific department from outside the university, you will need to obtain the appropriate P.O. Box Number and use the following address format:

Individual Name
Department
Georgia State University
P.O. Box XXXX
Atlanta, GA 30302-XXXX

Note: The four-digit P.O. Box number is also the four-digit zip code extension.

For courier and overnight delivery services such as FedEx and UPS, contact the recipient to obtain a physical address.
